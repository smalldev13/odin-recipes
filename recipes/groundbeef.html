Ingredients

  • 6 cups of water
  • 6 ounces of dry lentils
  • 1 onion
  • garlic, paprika, bay leaf, cumin, salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil in a pot.
  2. Stir lentils into water, reduce heat to low, and cook at a simmer, stirring occasionally, until the water is mostly absorbed, 25 to 25 minutes.
  3. Stir onion, garlic, paprika, bay leaf, cumin, salt, and pepper into the lentils; continue cooking until the water is fully absorbed and the lentils tender, 5 to 10 minutes more.
  4. Stir olive oil into the lentils. Remove and discard the bay leaf.
